# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Parallel Worlds to be a project where people can learn, build, and collaborate without being demeaned or pushed aside.

Examples of behavior that helps:

- being respectful in technical disagreement
- assuming good intent while staying honest
- welcoming contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels
- offering feedback on code and ideas, not on people

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insulting language
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, please contact the maintainers privately through the security/contact channel documented in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
